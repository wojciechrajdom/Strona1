Madeira

The island of eternal spring

The island is the top of a massive submerged shield volcano that rises about 6 km (3.7 mi)
from the floor of the Atlantic Ocean.

Olá Mundo!

Madeira was claimed by Portuguese sailors in the service of Prince Henry the Navigator in 1419 and settled after 1420.

The archipelago is considered to be the first territorial discovery of the exploratory period of the Age of Discovery.

Informations

Economy

The Gross domestic product (GDP) of the region was 4.9 billion euros in 2018, accounting for 2.4% of Portugal's economic output. GDP per capita adjusted for purchasing power was 22,500 euros or 75% of the EU27 average in the same year. The GDP per employee was 71% of the EU average.

Nature

Madeira island is home to several endemic plant and animal species. In the south, there is very little left of the indigenous subtropical rainforest that once covered the whole island.

Sport

Football is the most popular sport in Madeira. The island is the birthplace of international star Cristiano Ronaldo and is home to two prominent Primeira Liga teams, C.S. Marítimo - the only island team to win a national championship - and C.D. Nacional.
